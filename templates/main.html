This classfier was trained with data from the car data set at https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Car+Evaluation. It takes six attributes, shown above - initial buying price, maintenance cost, number of doors, passenger capacity, luggage capacity, and overall safety. Based on these six attributes, a car is classified as either unacceptable, acceptable, good, or very good. Strings have been converted to numbers for ease of input, with guides explaining what numbers mean for each feature.
